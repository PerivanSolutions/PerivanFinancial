Once the pack is ready to go, the administrator can simply print the pack for those who prefer printed copies, or publish the pack to the portal to only those who should receive it. The recipient will then automatically be alerted by email of its availability.

If, for any reason, the pack needs to be withdrawn, it can simply be unpublished at a click.

Shared documents

Each Virtualboardroom contains a shared documents area specific to the board or committee. This can contain any relevant generic corporate governance information that will not necessarily be included in the board pack.
